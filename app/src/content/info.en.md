## No account needed

No signup, no password, no phone number. The first time you open few.chat, a private identity is created right on your device.

## Your messages are private

Every message is end-to-end encrypted. Only you and the person you're writing to can read it — no one else, not even few.chat.

## Adding contacts

Share your personal link with someone you know. Once you are connected, you can message each other directly.

## Groups

Group chats are encrypted the same way, so only the members can read what is shared.

## Your data stays with you

Your name and avatar are never posted publicly. Your profile is shared only with the people you choose, and your data lives on your device.

## Free

few.chat is completely free. No ads, no tracking.

## Built on open protocols

few.chat is built on Nostr, an open messaging protocol — nothing here is proprietary. Direct messages are sealed with NIP-17 gift-wrap encryption, which hides both the content and who is talking to whom. Group chats use MLS, a modern encryption standard for secure messaging. Because the protocols are open, anyone can inspect exactly how few.chat protects your messages.
